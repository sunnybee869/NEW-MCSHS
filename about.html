Who We Are

We are committed to raising disciplined, knowledgeable, and morally sound students prepared for higher education and life.

Our school provides a balanced high school experience through excellent teaching, practical learning, moral instruction, leadership training, sports, music, clubs, science, and digital innovation.

  • Experienced teachers and structured classroom management
  • Science labs, ICT lab, library, and safe learning environment
  • Guidance and counselling support for students
  • Clubs, competitions, sports, leadership and community service
